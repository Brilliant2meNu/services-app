Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept the following payment methods:
    • Cash
    • Venmo: @tomikochans
    • Zelle: 310.508.4898
    • Debit/Credit Card
  • 2. How early should I arrive for my appointment?
    Please arrive 10 minutes early to complete any necessary intake forms and to ensure your treatment begins on time.
  • 3. What happens if I’m late to my appointment?
    Late arrivals will receive the remaining time of their scheduled appointment and will be charged the full price for the service.
  • 4. What is your rescheduling policy?
    We require 24-48 hours’ notice prior to your appointment to avoid being charged the full price of the booked services. If less than 24-48 hours’ notice is given due to sickness or a family emergency, you may reschedule your appointment without penalty.
  • 5. What is your policy on no-call/no-show appointments?
    A no-call/no-show is when a client neither shows up for their appointment nor informs us in advance. In such cases, the card on file will be charged 50% of the service price. Multiple no-call/no-show instances may result in being unable to book future appointments with us.
  • 6. Can I bring guests or children to my appointment?
    Only two adults over the age of 18 are allowed in the studio during a service. If the client is a minor under 18, a parent or guardian may be present. Children are not permitted in the studio unless they are receiving services.
  • 7. What is your cell phone policy?
    To ensure a relaxing environment, please make all calls and texts before your appointment and set your phone to silent during the session.
  • 8. Is there a policy regarding personal property?
    We are not responsible for lost or stolen property. Please leave valuable items at home.
  • 9. Do you have a cancellation policy?
    Yes, we require a 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations within 24 hours of the appointment may incur a fee.
  • 10. What are your hygiene and sanitation practices?
    We adhere to strict hygiene protocols, including regular disinfection of tools and workstations, to ensure the safety and well-being of our clients and staff.
  • 11. How can I book an appointment?
    Appointments can be booked online through our website or by contacting us directly.
  • 12. What is your refund policy?
    Refunds are not provided for services rendered. However, if you are dissatisfied, please inform us during your appointment so we can address any concerns.
  • 13. Do you accept gratuity?
    Gratuities are appreciated but not required. If you choose to leave a tip, it can be added to your payment method or given in cash.
  • 14. What should I do if I need to cancel due to illness?
    If you are unwell, please contact us as soon as possible. We understand that illnesses can arise suddenly, and we will work with you to reschedule your appointment.
  • 15. Are there any guidelines for first-time clients?
    First-time clients should arrive at least 10 minutes early to complete intake forms and discuss any specific needs or concerns with the therapist.
  • 16. How do you handle late arrivals?
    Clients arriving within a 5-15 minute grace period may receive an abbreviated service to keep the schedule on track. Arrivals beyond this may need to be rescheduled and could incur a late-cancellation fee.
  • 17. What safety protocols are in place?
    We follow industry-standard safety protocols, including the use of personal protective equipment and strict sanitation procedures, to ensure a safe environment for both clients and staff.
  • 18. What should I do if I’m not satisfied with my service?
    Please inform us during your appointment if you have any concerns so we can address them promptly.
  • 19. Do you offer gift certificates?
    Yes, gift certificates are available for purchase. Please contact us for more details.
