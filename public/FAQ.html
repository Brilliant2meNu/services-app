Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept cash, Venmo (@tomikochans), Zelle (310.508.4898), and debit/credit cards.
  • 2. How early should I arrive for my appointment?
    Arrive 10 minutes early to complete intake forms and ensure your treatment begins on time.
  • 3. What happens if I’m late to my appointment?
    Late arrivals receive the remaining time of their appointment and are charged the full price.
  • 4. What is your rescheduling policy?
    24-48 hours’ notice is required to avoid charges. Emergencies are considered.
  • 5. What is your policy on no-call/no-show appointments?
    50% of the service price is charged for no-call/no-shows. Repeated offenses may result in restrictions.
  • 6. Can I bring guests or children to my appointment?
    Two adults over 18 may accompany clients. Children are not permitted unless receiving services.
  • 7. What is your cell phone policy?
    Phones must be silent during appointments to maintain a relaxing environment.
  • 8. Do you have a cancellation policy?
    Cancellations require 24-hour notice to avoid fees.
  • 9. What are your hygiene and sanitation practices?
    We follow strict hygiene protocols, including disinfecting tools and workstations.
  • 10. How can I book an appointment?
    Appointments can be booked online or directly through our contact channels.
  • 11. Do you offer gift certificates?
    Yes, gift certificates are available. Contact us for details.
